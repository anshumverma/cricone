Payment History Workflow Test

Test Objective

Verify that the payment history detail view works correctly with real data flow:

  1. Upload Excel file with payment data
  2. Click on a player row in the table
  3. Verify payment history modal displays correctly
  4. Confirm all payments are shown with correct details
  5. Verify chronological sorting
  6. Confirm total amount paid is accurate

Step 3: Test Payment History Modal

Test Checklist:
  • Click on "John Smith" row:
    • Modal should open
    • Should show 3 payments
    • Payments should be sorted: Nov 15, Dec 15, Jan 15
    • Total amount paid should be $300.00
    • Each payment should show date, amount ($100.00), and plan (Monthly)
  • Click on "Sarah Johnson" row:
    • Modal should open
    • Should show 2 payments
    • Payments should be sorted: Oct 1, Jan 1
    • Total amount paid should be $500.00
    • Each payment should show date, amount ($250.00), and plan (Quarterly)
  • Click on "Mike Davis" row:
    • Modal should open
    • Should show 1 payment
    • Payment date: Jun 1, 2023
    • Total amount paid should be $1000.00
    • Payment should show plan (Annual)

Step 4: Verify Modal Functionality

Additional Tests:
  • Click the X button to close the modal - should close
  • Click outside the modal (on the dark overlay) - should close
  • Open modal for different players - should update content correctly
